# 📊 East Colleague Training Feedback Analysis

## 🎯 Project Overview
This project focuses on the analysis of feedback data collected from East Colleague training sessions. The objective is to evaluate participant engagement, identify satisfaction trends, and extract actionable insights to enhance future learning experiences.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
* **Microsoft Excel**: Data Cleaning, Pivot Tables, and Interactive Dashboards.

## 💡 Key Insights
* **Participant Demographics**: The participant base is primarily concentrated in Addis Ababa and Oromia, with a significant majority identifying as male (72.9%).
* **Engagement & Accessibility**: Mobile data is the dominant method for internet connectivity, and smartphones are the primary device for accessing training.
* **Outreach Effectiveness**: Telegram is the clear leader for both program discovery and ongoing communication, with a 95.8% perceived effectiveness rating among users.
* **Participation Drivers & Barriers**: "Certification upon completion" is the highest-rated driver for active engagement (33 votes), while "Lack of Time" and "Poor Internet Connection" are the most significant barriers to participation.
* **Requested Content**: There is a high demand for "Personal Development & Self-Transformation" (20.0%) and "Business, Sales & Entrepreneurship" (12.6%) tracks.

## 🚀 Methodology
* **Data Cleaning**: Standardized feedback formats, addressed missing values, and ensured consistency across datasets.
* **Analysis**: Utilized Pivot Tables to categorize responses and identify key performance indicators (KPIs).
* **Visualization**: Developed a dynamic, interactive dashboard to present trends clearly to stakeholders.
